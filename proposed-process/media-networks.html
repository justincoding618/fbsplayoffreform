Proposed Media Networks

Why Multi-Network Distribution Matters

Distributing playoff games across multiple networks maximizes revenue for schools and conferences while ensuring broad fan access regardless of cable provider or streaming service. A 32-team playoff generates 31 games across five rounds, which is far more inventory than any single network could handle. By partnering with ESPN/ABC, TNT Sports, FOX, CBS, NBC, and their streaming platforms, the playoff reaches the widest possible audience and creates competitive bidding that drives up rights fees. This model mirrors the NFL's approach, which has proven enormously successful in maximizing both revenue and viewership.
