Additional Adjustments

The regular season adjustments making room for up to a 14-game regular season schedule with the elimination of conference championship games would likely satisfy the modernization of college football (with up to 12 out of the 14 games being conference games). Since the Division I FCS, Division II, and Division III and do not enforce conference playoffs, implementing conference playoffs for Division I FBS seems unlikely. Conference playoffs would only happen if a drastic change to the FBS happens (such as the power conferences splitting away from the non-power FBS conferences, which I hope it doesn't happen).

The Army-Navy Dilemma

We would likely have to deal with the Army-Navy stand-alone if we want to make adjustments to the regular season. While still eliminating the conference championship games (and never implementing conference play-in games), there are two simple solutions that we can implement.
