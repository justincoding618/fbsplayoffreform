The 2026 season is the third season of the 12-team CFP. This is the second season where the CFP used straight seeding. It is also the first season of the rebooted Pac-12 Conference.

The final CFP rankings was used to determine National Seeding, and then the rest of the CFP Rankings and merit-based selection were used to determine the rest of the field.

The 32-team playoff field will be released following the conclusion of the 2026 Army-Navy rivalry game.
