Playoff Appearances

Note: The information regarding the 32-team playoff appearances by each team are current as of the most recent completed season. Content is subject to change depending on the given most recent completed season.

While looking at the 32-team fields since 1998, a total of 122 teams would have appeared at least once. One school that would have appeared in the 32-team field is no longer in the FBS. Among current Division I FBS teams, 18 teams have never made an appearance in the 32-team field, including 2 that are ineligible due to transitioning from FCS to FBS. In addition, for 2020, for teams that appear in both the 16-team and 32-team field, a double star (**) will appear, while teams that appear in only the 32-team field would get a single star (*).

Teams listed below are under their current athletic branding as well as their current conference affiliation, which are not necessarily what were used at the time. For examples:

  • Oregon and USC made playoff appearances while as Pac-10/Pac-12 members and as Big Ten members.
  • Nebraska made playoff appearances while as a Big 12 member and as a Big Ten member.
  • Missouri, Oklahoma, Texas, and Texas A&M made playoff appearances while as Big 12 members and as SEC members.
